Marcus Aurelius

-was a Roman emperor from 161 to 180 and a Stoic philosopher, He also wrote the Meditations. He was the last of the rulers known as the Five Good Emperors. The Meditations is a series of personal writings by Marcus, recording his private notes to himself and ideas on. He wrote the 12 books of the Meditations as a source for his own guidance and self-improvement.


“You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength.” -Marcus Aurelius
